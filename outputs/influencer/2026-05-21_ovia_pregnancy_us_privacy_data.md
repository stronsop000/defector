### Kinedu Influencer/Partner Brief: Privacy & Peace of Mind

**1. The angle**
Kinedu is where parents find trusted early development guidance without worrying about their personal data being shared or misused.

**2. Why this matters to parents right now**
Right now, parents are feeling really protective of their family's privacy. They're tired of apps that share sensitive info with partners you can't opt out of, or demand access to things like contacts and location for no clear reason. It's heartbreaking when private moments, like logging a miscarriage, lead to targeted ads for grief support.

**3. 4-6 talking points**

*   **Your data is *your* data.** We believe what happens in your family stays in your family. Kinedu doesn't share your sensitive information with third-party partners.
*   **No creepy tracking.** You won't find us asking for unnecessary permissions like your contacts or 24/7 location. We only collect what we need to make Kinedu work for *you*.
*   **You're in control.** Easily manage your account and data preferences right within the app. If you need to delete your account, it's simple and straightforward.
*   **A safe space for sensitive moments.** We understand that parenting journeys have ups and downs. Kinedu is designed to support you without ever turning your private experiences into targeted ads.
*   **Focus on what matters: your child's development.** Kinedu is all about giving you expert-backed activities to support your little one's growth, without the worry of how your personal info is being used.

**4. Sample hook line**
Want a parenting app that *actually* respects your privacy? Meet Kinedu.

**5. What NOT to say**
*   Do NOT mention competitor apps by name.
*   Do NOT make definitive medical claims or guarantee specific developmental outcomes.
*   Do NOT claim Kinedu collects *zero* data (we collect what's necessary for the service).

### Kinedu Influencer/Partner Brief: Ditch the Tech Frustration

**1. The angle**
Ditch the frustrating tech and embrace an app that reliably supports your child's development, making parenting a little easier.

Parents are busy and stressed. They need tools that simplify life, not add more headaches. They're tired of "errors out something went wrong" and apps that "read the brand wrong half the time."

**3. 4-6 talking points**
*   "Finally, an app that just *works*! No crashes, no 'something went wrong' messages when you're trying to get things done."
*   "Super easy to navigate and find exactly what you need, even when you only have a minute. No more fighting with frustrating tech."
*   "Kinedu gives you personalized activities and expert guidance to support your child's development, right from your phone."
*   "Spend less time troubleshooting frustrating apps and more time connecting with your little one, knowing you're supporting their growth."
*   "All activities are backed by developmental science, so you can feel confident you're doing what's best for your child."

**4. Sample hook line**
Parenting is hard enough. Your apps shouldn't be.

**5. What NOT to say**
*   Do not mention Huggies Rewards or any specific competitor by name.

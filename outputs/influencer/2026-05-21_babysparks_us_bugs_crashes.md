### Kinedu Influencer & Partner Brief

**1. The angle**
Kinedu makes supporting your baby's development easy and reliable, without the tech headaches.

**2. Why this matters to parents right now**
Parents are already juggling so much, the last thing they need is an app that crashes every time they open a video, gets stuck on a splash screen, or wipes their logged milestones. You just want something that works reliably on your iPad, so you can focus on connecting with your little one, not troubleshooting tech or cycling through password screens.

**3. 4-6 talking points**
*   Our app is built for stability – no more videos crashing or getting stuck on loading screens when you're ready to play.
*   Access all your activities and progress seamlessly on any device, including your iPad, without frustrating display issues.
*   Your baby's precious milestones and progress are safe and sound, logged securely without fear of them being wiped.
*   Forget endless password cycles or "session expired" messages – Kinedu offers a seamless login and user experience.
*   Spend less time troubleshooting and more time enjoying meaningful, expert-designed activities with your little one.
*   Hundreds of play-based activities designed by child development experts, right at your fingertips.

**4. Sample hook line**
Baby app not working? This one actually does!

**5. What NOT to say**
Avoid naming specific competitor apps or making direct negative claims about them. Focus on the *positive* experience Kinedu offers, without promising specific developmental outcomes.
